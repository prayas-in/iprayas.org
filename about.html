WHAT IS PRAYAS

PRAYAS stands for Program Associating Youth AndSociety. It is a modest attempt to involve individuals in societal issues.PRAYAS has twin objectives of creating awareness among the youth of social reality on the one hand and finding solutions to the problems ofÂ  society.

Sustainable Initiatives

We need to be clear while designing or executing any of our initiatives or programs. It is very important to keep the basic values in mind while designing a new initiative. Basically it needs to be addressing one or more of the social, economic and environmental development of the area. We should have a sustainable plan for it for the minimum of 5 years. It should be first tried and tested with a lesser area of action, after which it can be categorized into our sustainable initiatives.

VISION AND MISSION

We aim to provide individuals with a platform to ensure the fulfillment of Sustainable Development Goals for the weaker sections of the Society through sustainable initiatives.

PRAYAS exists to motivate the individuals and provide them with resources to solve the social problems of their surroundings by channelizing the local resources.

 

About our foundetion

We Are In A Mission To Help Helpless

LIn the vision statement we have two very important terms which will determine our activities in the long run, namely platform and sustainable initiatives. As we can see that we want to find out some sustainable solutions to the social problems and ensure its execution through a platform.
So let us understand what do we mean by sustainable initiatives. We want to develop such initiatives which ensure the improvement of the social, economic and environmental conditions of an area and last long. We aim to make them sustainable by using local resources and long term impact. Then after being able to successfully develop and implement some potential solutions in our headquarter, i.e. Kharagpur, we should try to implement those solutions throughout the country. This will be done through a platform which will be made to ensure proper implementation of the solutions and help to contribute to collective growth of each node of the network. In this platform we will be targeting the working individuals

Mrinal Kanti Bhanja

Creative Director

Our founder and CFO Mr. Mrinal Kanti Bhanja notices that the working individuals are ignorant of social problems. Most of them are migrating outside from the community. He believes that if these people are given the right direction and platform, they can solve the common prevalent social problems of their community using locally available resources. He identified two reasons behind this feeble social participation of people:
Firstly, people are under the chains of their financial insecurities. They think that doing social work will not allow them to make their living and hence they lack motivation and passion to bring a change.
Secondly, even if some are motivated they lack a platform and proper guidance and a team of like-minded people to execute their ideas to solve social problems.
Through this initiative, we want to motivate people to join us and find out ways to develop an area using the locally available resources.
