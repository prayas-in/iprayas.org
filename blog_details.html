Explanation of Vision and Mission Statements
In the vision statement we have two very important terms which will determine our activities in the long run, namely platform and sustainable initiatives. As we can see that we want to find out some sustainable solutions to the social problems and ensure its execution through a platform.
So let us understand what do we mean by sustainable initiatives. We want to develop such initiatives which ensure the improvement of the social, economic and environmental conditions of an area and last long. We aim to make them sustainable by using local resources and long term impact. Then after being able to successfully develop and implement some potential solutions in our headquarter, i.e. Kharagpur, we should try to implement those solutions throughout the country. This will be done through a platform which will be made to ensure proper implementation of the solutions and help to contribute to collective growth of each node of the network. In this platform we will be targeting the working individuals

 

What is PRAYAS
PRAYAS stands for Program Associating Youth AndSociety. It is a modest attempt to involve individuals in societal issues.PRAYAS has twin objectives of creating awareness among the youth of social reality on the one hand and finding solutions to the problems of  society.

  1. Initial Programs

            We have identified some initiatives which can make a longer impact on society.
Following are the initiatives under PRAYAS:

    • BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM
    • INTERNSHIP PROGRAM
    • LIVE PROJECT
    • GLOBAL WARMING
    • EARN A SMILE

    2.1 BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM
    It is an initiative which supports social entrepreneurship by interested entrepreneurs. We believe that social enterprises can prove to be pioneers in community development. Under this program we will be helping people to set up businesses and also grow their business. The team will be proficient with the business processes and will be focusing on developing strategies as per the requirements of the associated businesses.

    2.2 INTERNSHIP PROGRAM
    In this initiative creative minds from various institutes are chosen for identifying issues of society and finding the respective solution for the same. Youth and creative mind from different institute will be selected and these youth will be trained by highly qualified experienced Professors of IIT Kharagpur

    2.3 LIVE PROJECT
    It is the channel to put the budding managers on the track of social work. The project involves selecting the potential candidates from various institutes. Giving them the liberty to work on a problem and expanding the scope of the work to adoption of a particular region and support the region in its development.

    2.4 GLOBAL WARMING
    This program will address the environmental aspect of development. Global Warming is an alarming phenomena which causes the gradual heating of Earth’s surface. This program aims at taking steps and making people aware about the steps which can reduce global warming and its effects.

    2.5 EARN A SMILE
    Under this initiative, we aim to provide the underprivileged and homeless children with education, food and hostel facilities. Basically, we will make a Child Education Fund under which anyone can contribute for the education of needy children.

     

    BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM:
    List of contents –

    1. Overview
    2. Objective

    Objective
    The objective of this program is to identify business enthusiasts  or entrepreneurs and help them to grow their enterprise . we will make them realise about the social aspect of the program and develop a channel through which they can give back to society.
    The program has three objectives-

    1. Helping entrepreneurs set up their own enterprise , to help raise standard of living for themselves and their family.
    2. Generate employment for the community through the enterprise set up.
    3. Instill a sense of gratitude and social responsibility in everyone associated with the program.

    Vision
    We aim to nurture socially motivated and dedicated entrepreneurs by providing them workforce, resources, and strategies to set up and expand their own enterprise and generate employment.

     

    Segments under the program

    1. Help an existing business: under this section , we will collaborate with the existing  local business and help them to gain a bigger audience and markets . we will provide them.
    2. Help individuals with startup ideas to set up a fresh startup or business- we can help the entrepreneur by providing some initial funds and other resources to  develop the product . We can also find some programs which help such innovative business ideas.
    3. Develop a business model – We can also work on some business models and help  it to gain a significant structure. For this we can hire interns or before skilled individuals who are ready to work on ideas. Although , during the process or before the registration of the company we shall find someone who will be taking over the business as his own.

    Some ideas that have been proposed are as follows –

    1. HR consultancy – maintain a record of locals looking for jobs like housemaids, labourers, etc. and provide them employment opportunities as and when available.
    2. Education consultancy – This is related to making a platform where students can find relevant data of several colleges based on several trends.
    3. Education/Company consultancy
    4. Info- Tech
    5. Training/Internship program
    6. Merchandise and e-commerce

    Procedure
    Any individual or company willing to register under PRAYAS need to submit a business  proposal in a predefined format. After receiving the proposal, it shall be reviewed by the PRAYAS team. The review process comprises judging the feasibility of the proposal and developing a strategy to be implemented. After the plan of action has been developed, our executive team shall take care of the execution of the action plan.

     

    INTERNSHIP PROGRAM
    AIM: To encourage dynamic college students to join hands to work for underprivileged & weaker sections of the society. Usher new leadership and team spirit qualities among the young students. Build up a platform which would help in taking the GYWS name all over the country.

     

    EXECUTION:

    1. Initiating the program under this team with the Coordinators, Heads, Senior Executive Member and Junior Executive Member
    2. Make the list of the target institutes.
    3. Design the poster of the program mentioning important dates.
    4. Post the advertisement in Internshala, LetsIntern and other internship portals.
    5. Attempt to connect the appropriate persons of the institutes.
    6. Initiation of the process in the accepted institutes.
    7. Preparation of the selection criteria of the interns.
    8. Search for the resource persons.
    9. Preparation of the two day schedule.
    10. Inviting the dignitaries for the two days training program inauguration function.
    11. Design of the banner for the two day training program.
    12. Telephonic interview for the candidates applied.
    13. Invitation to the candidates selected.
    14. Execution of the two days training program.
    15. Keeping track of the interns.
    16. Design of the certificates for the interns.
    17. One day presentation by the interns at IIT.
    18. Issue of the certificates.

    TIMELINE OF EVENTS EVERY FINANCIAL YEAR:

    1. July: posture design, application questionnaire and contacts of the colleges.
    2. August: fix the resource persons.
    3. August: approaching colleges.
    4. August: application process initiation.
    5. September: last date for application.
    6. September: shortlist of interns and intimated through the call, msg, and email.
    7. October: intern to IIT.
    8. November: survey and college, schools contacts and parallel collection of funds.
    9. December: design of the program with budget.
    10. January: collection of funds must be completed.
    11. February: completing the program designed.
    12. March: submission of the report.
    13. March last week: issue of the certificates.

     

    BASIC QUALIFICATIONS OF THE INTERN:

    1. Pursuing Bachelor’s or Master’s degree program in any reputed institute.
    2. An understanding of and passion to work for society.
    3. Good communication skills, fluency in English, Hindi or other national languages will be an added advantage.
    4. Good convincing skills .
    5. Ability to work in team
    6. Excellent organizational, analytical and problem solving skills.

    SELECTION CRITERIA FOR THE HEAD :

    1. Worked in the team for one year.
    2. Participate in the entire process of the program.
    3. Attend all the meetings held within the team.
    4. Be active throughout the financial year and attend all the activities of the GYWS.

    SELECTION CRITERIA FOR SENIOR EXECUTIVE MEMBER:

    1. Worked in the team for one year.
    2. Participation in some of the activities of the program.
    3. Be active throughout the financial year and attend all the activities of the GYWS.

     

    LIVE PROJECT PROGRAM
    AIM: To provide a platform for the young managers to prove themselves as social leaders and to give some input in solving the problems bothering the weaker sections of the society. Give them the liberty to work on a problem and expand the scope of the work to adoption of a particular region and support the region in its development. Make the youth aware of societal problems. Provide an opportunity for interns to be trained by the IIT professors. Build up a platform which would help in taking the GYWS name all over the country.

    EXECUTION:

    1. Management interns will be selected from premier B-School, we will refer to them as Managers
    2. They need to submit their preference work area, chosen for project
    3. Management Intern will start work with hiring Interns, by posting the openings in Internshala, LetsIntern and other internship portals
    4. Managers need to select and submit the details of the interns, after the selection process and interviews (Process is not specific, it is flexible to the manager)
    5. Knowledge dissemination and training need to be given by Managers to General Interns 
    6. General Interns go to various institutes and present about GYWS.
    7. He/ she raises funds from the institutes and from all the other possible sources using the form given by GYWS for the project he is assigned to.
    8. He/ she conducts the survey on the local problems in their locality.
    9. He/ she conducts a social awareness program or some other solution initiative through the funds he raised.

    CONCLUSION:

    1. If Manager intern  completes the assignment, he will receive the certificate of completion for the live project
    2. If an intern hired by a manager accomplishes this task, gets the Internship certificate from GYWS.

    TIMELINE OF EVENTS EVERY FINANCIAL YEAR:
    This internship program will be happening in the odd semester and begin in July’2015.

    1. July 15-July 30:  GYWS will be hiring managers who drive the events .They will be deciding the location of their choice.
    2. August 1- August 10:  Managers will be hiring interns on the location they have chosen and they will be given insight about the internship program. They will be told about their duties and responsibilities. Besides they will be given training by some of the IIT faculties.
    3. August 11- August 30: Then Interns will be researching about the problems prevailing in nearby areas and also be building databases about the schools. They need to submit a detailed database of the school contacts which includes address, contact number, number of students in school etc. Intern needs to conduct a survey wherein he will get to know the problems prevailing in the society.
    4. September 1- September 15:   Interns will start contacting schools for raising funds and they need to tell the progress they have made till now. Mid-term report will be prepared by the managers and managers continuously need to evaluate the interns. They also need to motivate them to convert the schools to join in the campaign by GYWS
    5. September 16- October 15:  Interns will be carrying over with the work of raising funds from the schools by meeting and convincing school officials. The intern also needs to meet the students of the schools and make them aware of this campaign. This will bring into the notice of youth about the problems around them and how they can contribute to resolve them. Intern also needs to start preparing for the event which will be addressing the issue of that area.
    6. October 16- October 31:  The intern will arrange the event which can run for 2-3 days and need to run this event under the banner of GYWS. This event should later conduct a survey to get the feedback whether the event really addressed the issue which is prevailing in the society?
    7. November 1-November 10:  Managers will then have to submit the detailed report of the program to GYWS.

    GLOBAL WARMING

    AIM : Its goal is to limit global warming to well below 2, preferably two 1.5 degrees Celsius, compared to two pre-industrial levels . To achieve this long term temperature goal, countries aim to reach global peaking of greenhouse gas emission as soon as possible to achieve a climate neutral world by midcentury.

    EXECUTION :

    1. Plantation
    2. Seminar and workshop on climate change.
    3. Rain water Harvesting.
    4. Awareness of Industrial pollution.
    5. Solution of water and air pollution and reduce it.
    6. Switch to renewable energy.
    7. Turn off electronic devices.
    8. Reducing the carbon dioxide in the atmosphere.

    The programs will be chosen from the above mentioned ideas depending upon the area and the resources available. These can be conducted in the IIT Kharagpur chapter as well as any other PRAYAS chapters.

    CONCLUSION :
    We propose the following solutions should be implemented as soon as possible.
    First , there must be an international political solution.
    Second, funding for developing cheap and clean energy production must be increased, as all economic development is based on increasing energy usage. We must not pin all our hopes on global politics and clean energy technology, so we must prepare for the worst and adapt . if implemented now, a lot of the costs and damage that could be caused by changing climate can be mitigated.

           
    EARN A SMILE
    AIM: India is home to millions of poor people who have limited means in life. Some even struggle to get the basic needs in life, viz. food, water, clothing and shelter. Despite considerable progress made in the socio economic sphere since independence, even today, more than 23.4% of India’s  population lives below the poverty line, as per the World Bank figures.
    Those children who belong to the below poverty line, do not have access to education and good food and shelter. Through this initiative we want to shape their future for good and earn a smile on those children’s faces.

    EXECUTION:

    1. We will identify such children who are not going to school or not having home
    2. We will get them admitted to a school and bear all the expense of their education
    3. We will provide them with books, uniforms and school bags to study.
    4. The homeless children will be shifted to hostels and good food will be provided to them.
    5. We will be monitoring the progress of every child through our volunteers and the weak students will be provided with necessary coaching.
    6. We will also focus on the all round development of the children.
    7. Drop out candidates will also get all the educational facilities.

    CONCLUSION:

      • Through this program we will be able to make a living for all the children associated with us through our educational or skill development programs
      • Some children have to drop out due to lack of resources or family pressure. Through this program we will be able to reduce this.
